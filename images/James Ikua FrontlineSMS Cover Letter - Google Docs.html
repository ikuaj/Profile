Dear Shiro Theuri, 
My name is James Ikua. I am thrilled to be applying for the position of an Android Developer at 
FrontLineSMS​. I came across this job posting through the business development manager at 
Moringa School, and after reviewing it, I undoubtedly consider my skills a perfect match for 
your needs. 
My fascination with computers began when I was 11 and I helped my brother fix an old 
computer. Since then, I’ve always been an avid gamer who loves solving challenging problems 
and helping out where I can. I got mesmerised by the work I saw from your website and that 
prompted me to learn more about your company and current openings. From my research it is 
clear that you are looking for an enthusiastic full stack developer who is reliable and can 
effectively build solid software. Given these requirements, I believe that I am the perfect 
candidate for the job. 
My life long journey of coding began at Moringa school​,​ since then I have developed websites 
until I decided to turn the tables around and learn Android development. It was such an amazing 
experience and also highly educative. I had the opportunity to study languages such as HTML, 
CSS, Javascript, Java and front-end based frameworks such as Angular and Spark. I am eager to 
learn more languages and meet new people. 
Please review the enclosed resume and references, and consider my application. I am excited to 
elaborate on how my skills and abilities will benefit your organization​. 
Unfeignedly, 
James Ikua.
